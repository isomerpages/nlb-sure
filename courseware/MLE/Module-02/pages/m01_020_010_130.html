Summary

  • The intent of media messages may be direct or indirect. We have to uncover their intent to truly understand them.
  • A lot of effort goes into creating media messages. It is all about grabbing your attention and influencing your behaviour.
  • It is important to be a well-informed media consumer. This way you'll be in control of the messages that come your way and you won't be an easy target for influencing tactics.
