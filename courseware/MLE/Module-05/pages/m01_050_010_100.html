Summary

  • Being media literate also means being able to create media responsibly.
  • A typical media creation process involves:
  • Having the right goal
  • Understanding the audience
  • Gathering the right information
  • Choosing the appropriate media format
  • Designing the message
  • Reviewing and publishing
  • Care should be taken in each step of the process to avoid harming other people's culture, beliefs and values.
