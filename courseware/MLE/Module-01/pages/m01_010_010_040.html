Summary

  • The term 'the media' refers to the group of people or companies who put out messages using different media forms.
  • Media plays important roles in society. Its 4 key roles are to inform, educate, entertain and influence us.
  • The 3 concepts about media messages are that it is: constructed, has a purpose and is designed to influence.
