Summary

  • As you've seen in the examples, media messages can be interpreted differently by different people. The important point to bear in mind is that constant use of messages influences our thinking, values and beliefs. It is important therefore to analyse these messages carefully and understand their influence.
  • There are implications to long-term exposure to media messages.
  • The 5 key themes to be wary of are: beauty and body image, gender bias, culture and values, sexual overtones and violence.
  • Messages are sent for a purpose. They need to be critically analysed before you accept them.
