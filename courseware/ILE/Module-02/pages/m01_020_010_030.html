Problem Solving - A Universal Process

Let us review what Ms. Wong did to get to Surrey…

IL is knowing when and why you need information, where to find it, and how to evaluate, use and communicate it in an ethical manner.

Source: http://www.clip.org.uk/professionalguidance/informationliteracy/definition/

She had an information need – i.e. how to get to Surrey from London.

She sought information from a credible source.

She evaluated her options based on her planned itinerary.

She used the information and decided to buy a city-ticket that was considered economical and practical.

Once in Surrey, she assessed the outcome and concluded that she made the right choice in buying a city ticket.
