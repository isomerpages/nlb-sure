Introduction

In this module, you will learn about the popular Information Literacy (IL) models, such as the Big6™ and Empowering 8.

You will also learn about the IL programme developed by National Library Board (NLB).

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Explain the context of being information literate.

  • Differentiate between the Big6™ and Empowering 8 IL models.

  • Describe the stages in the IL programme developed by NLB.
