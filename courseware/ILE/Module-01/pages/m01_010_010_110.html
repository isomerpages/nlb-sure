How a Librarian can Help

Today, libraries are hybrid in nature. They provide access to physical and digital resources making information accessible almost anywhere, anytime. Libraries have become social learning spaces too.

Librarians are no longer managing resources but are now becoming navigators of information.

Librarians can help by:

  • Imparting Information Literacy skills to library patrons especially the younger generation.

  • Helping students locate relevant and timely information.

  • Guiding students to find suitable information and acquire information searching skills through project work.

  • Teaching students to evaluate information critically.
