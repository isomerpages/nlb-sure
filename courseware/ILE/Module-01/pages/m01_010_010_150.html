Summary

In this module you discovered that IL is a set of skills that enables a person to use information critically, solve problems, make decisions and become a lifelong learner. Students too, are being moulded to become critical thinkers and mature independent learners who will be responsible in society. To help realise this, NLB too, can help equip students with the necessary skills and competencies to become information literate. Educators and librarians can work together during the information gathering stage of project work.
