Introduction

In this module, you will learn about Information Literacy (IL) - what it means, why is it important and how you, as educators and librarians, can help in nurturing information literate students.

By the end of the module, you will be able to:

  • Define Information Literacy.

  • Explain the role of Information Literacy in the knowledge economy.

  • Explain how project work can be used for driving Information Literacy.

  • Explain how Information Literacy can be incorporated into project work.
