How Educators can Help

Today schools are focusing their curriculum on problem-based or resource-based learning. In addition, information technology has made more information readily available. Students today face the challenge of information overload.

Educators are moving away from simply imparting knowledge to facilitating learning.

Educators can help by:

  • Integrating IL in project work.

  • Guiding students to think critically when using information.

  • Advising students to present the information gathered in a coherent way.

  • Facilitating the students to reflect on what has been learnt from the experience.
