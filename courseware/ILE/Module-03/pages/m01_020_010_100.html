Third component of S.U.R.E.: Research

The third component of S.U.R.E. is to dig deeper and investigate the material you are reading.

Check the information you have found online with academic papers, books, reports to verify the information. To find resources from the National Library, you can use its new search service - NLB SearchPlus or eresources website.

URL for NLB SearchPlus: http://nlbsearchplus.nlb.gov.sg/primo_library/libweb/action/search.do?vid=SEARCHPLUS

URL for eresources website: http://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/index.aspx
