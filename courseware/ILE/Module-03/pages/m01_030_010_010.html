Introduction

In this module, you will go through a case study that will detail when and how educators and librarians can collaborate to make the project work assigned to students an experience in IL.

By the end of the module, you will be able to:

  • Identify the ways in which librarians and educators can collaborate during the course of project work.

  • Describe how you can contribute during the different IL phases.

  • Explain how the teacher and librarian can work together to implement IL.
