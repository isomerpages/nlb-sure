Resources

Here are some resources that may be useful to you in writing a report.

Books on research and research writing:

Writing website from Monash University:

A comprehensive website that has writing tips for reports as well as for essays.

It deconstructs many examples to show the principles behind good writing.

http://www.monash.edu.au/lls/llonline/writing/index.xml

Writing Lab at Purdue:

This is quite similar to the Monash University website.

http://owl.english.purdue.edu

E-learning course on Smart Report Writing:

Smart Research Report:

An e-learning course by NLB. Take this course to learn in detail on planning and writing a research report.

http://www.nlba.sg/courses.html

Books on research and research writing:

The following books describe the research process in detail:

Sharon Sorenson:

How to write research papers: easy to follow, practical instruction for high school and college students (3rd ed.).

Dr Christopher Hart:

Doing a Literature Search: A Comprehensive Guide for the Social Sciences (1st ed.).

Paul Leedy and Jeanne Ormrod:

Practical Research: Planning and Design (8th ed.).
