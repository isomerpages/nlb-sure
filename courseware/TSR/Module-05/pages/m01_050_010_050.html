Writing Checklist

10 Tips on Writing Well
1. You know your reader well Who is the audience for the report and what do they expect?
2. The research topic is well-defined Does the topic interest you? Can you narrow it down further?
3. Consider the breadth and depth of sources Have both qualitative/quantitative and primary/secondary dimensions been considered?
4. Evaluate sources Have all sources passed the evaluation criteria?
5. Reference sources Is your bibliography/reference list complete?
6. Assess the sources and note any gaps or opportunities Do you notice areas that can be focused on?
7. Analyse findings Do you see any patterns emerging?
Any areas that interest you?
8. Structure the research argument or stance in a logical manner Will others understand and follow your stance?
9. Make your perspective or voice stand out in the essay or report Is your perspective and rationale well-communicated?
10. Ensure that the essay or report is proof-read and reviewed by your team Have you checked and re-checked for errors and inconsistencies?
