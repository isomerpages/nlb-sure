Resources

Here are some resources that may be useful for using concept mapping and affinity diagramming.

Rapid problem solving with Post-It notes:
This is a good book that gives a simple step-by-step process on how to use Post-It notes for analysing information.

The Theory Underlying Concept Maps and How to Construct and Use Them:
This seminal paper describes all you need to know about concept maps and how to construct them.
It covers a lot of theory but you can skip to the practical part if you need to know how to
use them for analysing your research findings. http://cmap.ihmc.us/Publications/ResearchPapers/TheoryUnderlyingConceptMaps.pdf

What is affinity diagramming?
This article by Gerry Gaffney takes you through the basic principles of affinity diagramming.
http://www.infodesign.com.au/usabilityresources/general/affinitydiagramming.asp
