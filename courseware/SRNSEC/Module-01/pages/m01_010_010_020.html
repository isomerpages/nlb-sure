Lesson Outline

This lesson will cover the following:

  • Types of newspapers:
    • - General-interest and special-interest newspapers
    - Broadsheets and tabloids
  • Common terms associated with newspapers e.g. headline, byline, secondary lead, etc.
Learning Objectives

At the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify the features and coverage of newspapers in general
  • Explain terms associated with newspapers such as headline, byline, secondary lead, etc.
  • Differentiate between broadsheets and tabloids
  • Describe the coverage and target audience of the major newspapers in Singapore
