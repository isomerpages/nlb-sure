Skimming for Information

Skimming involves a quick read of the article to look for answers to the 5Ws. This will help you decide if the article is relevant for your project.

5Ws: As mentioned in the earlier module, answers to the 5W questions (i.e. What, What, Where, Who and Why) will provide factual information about the newspaper article.
