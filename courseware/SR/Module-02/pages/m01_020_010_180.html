Choosing a Research Method – Qualitative Approach

Qualitative comes from the word “Quality” or How good or bad?”

A qualitative approach to research means that we want to collect opinions - descriptive information.


It is opinion-based research. It involves observing, interviewing and analysing people’s behaviour and what they say.


Examples:

  • How do you feel about the gifted programme?
  • What are your opinions about blogging?
  • How do senior citizens feel about the recent increase in bus and train fares?

Tip

Your research method may also adopt a combination of the quantitative and qualitative approaches.
