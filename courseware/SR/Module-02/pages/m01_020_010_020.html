Lesson Outline

This lesson will cover the following:

  • The first 3 steps of the research process:
    • Step 1: Define the project topic and problem statement
    • Step 2: Identify the research objectives
    • Step 3: Choose a research method

Learning Objectives

At the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Define a project topic and craft your problem statement
  • Identify the research objectives for a given problem statement
  • Differentiate between quantitative and qualitative research
  • Differentiate between primary and secondary data collection
