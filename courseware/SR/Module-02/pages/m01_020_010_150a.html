Summary of the Research Objectives

What is the main aim of this research?

The main aim of this research is to find out whether Singaporean's awareness of SARs is low.


Who is our target population for this research?

Our target population is students and working adults.


What information do we need to collect to find out whether Singaporeans' awareness of SARS is low?

Feedback from a sample of the population on questions about SARS.


Why do we want this information?

If we can prove that awareness of SARS is low, then we can recommend relevant educational programmes to help increase awareness of SARS. This will help Singaporeans cope better in the event of a SARS epidemic in future.
