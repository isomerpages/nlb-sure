Step 2: Identify the Research Objectives

The research objectives are very important in helping the group choose a suitable research method and the type of questions to ask their target population.

In this step, the group needs to identify their research objectives by asking questions like:

  • What is the main aim of our research?
  • Who is our target population?
  • What information do we need?
  • Why do we want this information?
  • What will we do with the information that we find?
