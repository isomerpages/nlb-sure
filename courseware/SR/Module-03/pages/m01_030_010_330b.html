Other pitfalls in data analysis:
  • The question asked is not relevant. For example, from this question, we know how many people like chocolate, strawberry, banana and plain milk, but we do not know how this information relates to green tea-flavoured milk.
  • Asking too many questions. Not all questions are useful and having many questions means more work for you to collate and organise the data.
