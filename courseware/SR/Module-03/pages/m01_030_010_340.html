You should also be aware of the following ethical issues during data collection and analysis:

  • Do not manipulate the results to make them look good or impressive
    • Example: Making the first and second bars in the two bar charts (for Q2 and Q3) very short so that it is easier to prove that Singaporeans’ awareness of SARS is low
  • Do not plagiarise
    • Do not use other people’s data and analysis as your own
    • If you use data from other sources (secondary data), acknowledge the source and cite it in your report
