Questionnaires

The guidelines we have introduced in the section on survey questions apply to questionnaires as well:

  • Use closed-ended and open-ended questions
  • Avoid leading or loaded questions
  • Avoid double-barrelled questions
  • Are the respondents familiar with the issues you are asking them about
  • Sequence the easy, factual and general questions at the beginning of the questionnaire
  • Place the more difficult, sensitive and abstract questions towards the end of the questionaire

Keep these guidelines in mind as we proceed with our discussion on questionnaires.
