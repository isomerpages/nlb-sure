Summary

  • Plagiarism is copying somebody else's work and passing it off as your own.
  • It is still plagiarism if you copy someone else's idea, even though you did not copy his exact words.
  • Teachers are constantly looking out for plagiarised work.
  • Plagiarism is not a solution when you have no time to complete your work.
  • Common knowledge is information that many people know and express in a similar way.
