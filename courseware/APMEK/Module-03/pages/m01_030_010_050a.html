The Marina Reservoir is Singapore's 15th reservoir and the first city reservoir. It was created after a barrage was built across the Marina Channel, thereby separating the seawater from the water in the Marina Basin. It represents the first steps in making Singapore self-sufficient in its water supply. The current agreement with Malaysia to supply water ends in 2061. When fully operational in 2010 or 2011, the reservoir will be able to meet 10% of Singapore's water demands. The Marina Reservoir also stands to become a popular lifestyle destination with water sports like sailing and kayaking available year-round.
