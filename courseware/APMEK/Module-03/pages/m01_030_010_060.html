Summary

  • You are paraphrasing when you use somebody else's idea mentioned in a paragraph or paragraphs and rewrite it using your own words.
  • You are summarising when you use somebody else's idea mentioned in their book or article and rewrite it in your own words.
  • You must cite the source if you paraphrase or summarise using somebody else's idea.
