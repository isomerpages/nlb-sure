Summarising (Cont'd)

Summary of Al Gore's book:

According to Al Gore (2007), the ravages of global warming are too many to be ignored. The polar ice caps are melting at an alarming rate resulting in rising water levels, which are slowly killing the plant and animal life there. The changes at the poles are playing havoc with weather conditions elsewhere in the world. We are now seeing more flooding and more severe hurricane seasons as the years go by. Thousands of people have already lost their lives or their livelihood. The numbers are just going to rise if we don't act now!


Citation:

Gore, A. (2007). An Inconvenient Truth: The Crisis of Global Warming. Viking Juvenile.
