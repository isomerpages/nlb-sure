The Marina Reservoir is Singapore's 15th reservoir and the first city reservoir. It has the largest catchment area of 10,000 hectares. It was created after the Marina Barrage, a low-level dam, was built to separate seawater from the Marina Basin. Although the reservoir was commissioned on 31st October 2008, it will be used in the water supply system a year or two later when all the seawater in it is drained away (Chew, 2008).
