Summary

  • If you copy and use somebody else's exact words, then it is called quoting.
  • If you use a quote from somewhere and provide a reference to the source, it is called citing.
  • APA and MLA are two commonly used citation styles.
  • There are two methods of citation: in-text or short citation, and bibliographic or long citation.
  • Citation Machine is a website that allows you to easily create bibliographic citations for your source.
