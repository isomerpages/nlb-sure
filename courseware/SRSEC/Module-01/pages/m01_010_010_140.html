Stages of Project Work Reporting

We are now ready to learn how to write a research report. There are 3 stages to project work reporting. Do you know what they are?

Click each stage below to learn more.

Planning

Stage 1: Planning

  • Draft the outline of the report according to the 8 sections you learnt earlier

  • Gather all the information you have

  • Decide what needs to go into the report

Note: We will learn more about this stage in Modules 2 and 3 of this course.

Writing

Stage 2: Writing

  • Use appropriate report writing style

  • Your research report should be clear, structured, and factual. (Click here for CLASS guidelines).

Note: We will learn more about this stage in Module 4 of this course.

Presenting

Stage 3: Presenting

  • Prepare to present your project work to your teacher and classmates

  • Create presentation materials to communicate your project

  • Present effectively and with impact.
