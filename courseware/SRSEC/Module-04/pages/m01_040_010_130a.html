APA Citation Style for

Newspaper:
Ibrahim, F. (2005, November 15). Teenagers and buying habits. Teenage News, pp. H4.

Journal or Magazine:
Lau, D. (2005). Youth today recycle and reuse to generate income. Innovation Today, 15(3), 33-45.

Book:
Yeo, J. (2005). Research Methods: An introduction. 3rd ed. Singapore: Vision Books.

Webpage:
Munoo, R. (2005). Women and jewellery buying trends. Retrieved Feb. 08, 2006, from Women Entrepreneurs website: http://www.womene.com/jewellery

CD-ROM:
Pwee, T. (2005). How to make jewellery from recycled materials. In Design Encyclopaedia [CD-ROM]. Singapore: Vision Books.

MLA Citation Style for

Newspaper:
Ibrahim, Faridah. " Teenagers and buying habits." Teenage News 15 November 2005: H4.

Journal or Magazine:
Lau, Douglas. " Youth today recycle and reuse to generate income." Innovation Today June 2005: 33-45.

Book:
Yeo, Jeffrey. Research Methods: An introduction. 3rd ed. Singapore: Vision Books, 2005.

Webpage:
Munoo, Rajendra. " Women and jewellery buying trends." Women Entrepreneurs. 10 2005. Women Empowerment. 08 Feb. 2006 < http://www.womene.com/jewellery >.

CD-ROM:
Pwee, Timothy. " How to make jewellery from recycled materials." Design Encyclopaedia. CD-ROM. 2nd ed. 2005.
