The Research Methods Section

Brad’s group is ready to write The Research Methods Section.

TIP

The Research Methods Section usually includes the following:

  • What research methods were used?
  • Who is the target population?
  • How many people were surveyed?
  • The research process – where, when and how was the research conducted?
  • Why were these research methods used?
  • If questionnaires were used, how were they returned?
