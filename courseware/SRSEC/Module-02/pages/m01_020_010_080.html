How were the Questionnaires Returned?

This item is required in surveys involving questionnaires. If you send out survey questionnaires to people by post or through email, you need to explain how the completed questionnaires were sent back to you by the respondents.

Examples:

  • Through collection boxes placed at various locations
  • Through email
  • By post, using self-addressed envelopes.
