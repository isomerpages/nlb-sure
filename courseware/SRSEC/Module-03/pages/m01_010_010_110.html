Address the problem statement. State whether the findings :

  • confirm your hypothesis (i.e. assumption), or
  • provide an answer to the question made in your problem statement

3

State any deviation from expectation

  • If the results are different from your expectations, describe this in the conclusion.

4

Conclusions must be supported by evidence or data

  • Do not draw conclusions which are not supported by your results
