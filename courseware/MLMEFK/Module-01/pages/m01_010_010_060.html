Summary

  • TV, radio, newspapers, magazines and the Internet are called the Media.
  • Media is everywhere.
  • Media messages inform, educate, entertain and influence.
  • Not all messages are correct or appropriate. That is why Media messages needs to be controlled.
    That is also why you need to recognise these messages and make your own decisions about what is
    right and wrong, instead of accepting the messages blindly.
