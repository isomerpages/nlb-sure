Summary

  • Media messages are usually dressed up. For example, models wearing make-up, digitally altered images, exaggerating features or functions.
  • When you see a Media message, try to understand the reason why the message is dressed up and what its main goal is. The goal could be to inform, educate, or entertain. But remember that all messages are designed to influence.
