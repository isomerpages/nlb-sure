Two commonly used referencing styles:

Modern Language Association (MLA)

The MLA style is used by researchers for subjects in the Humanities.

American Psychological Association (APA)

The APA style is used by researchers for subjects in the Social Sciences.

In this module we will be looking at the MLA citation style.


Did you know?

Some institutions may also have their own in-house citation style-guides, such as the Harvard Style, Chicago, Columbia, and Turabian Styles.
