What is Plagiarism?

According to the Merriam-Webster’s Online Dictionary

http://www.m-w.com/dictionary/plagiarize

Plagiarize
  • To steal and pass off ideas or words of another as one’s own
  • To use another’s work without crediting the source
  • Present an idea derived from an existing source as new or original
  • To commit literary theft
