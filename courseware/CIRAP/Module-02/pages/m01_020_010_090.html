What is Skimming?

Skimming:

  • A quick read of a document
  • To determine the gist or main idea of the document

Skim to see if document is relevant to your research.

Click on the numbers for tips on skimming.

  • 1
    Tip #1:
    • Speed read and do not read for comprehension
    • Do not read word-for-word
  • 2
    Tip #2:
    • Read the title, subtitles and subheadings to find out what the document is about
  • 3
    Tip #3:
    • Look at the first and last paragraphs
    • These sections provide you with the gist of the article. Writers provide an introduction in the first paragraph and summarise their thoughts in the last paragraph.
