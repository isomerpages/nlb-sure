How to Write an In-Text Citation?

APA stands for American Psychological Association. It is one of the commonly-used citation styles.

For further details on the APA style, refer to the following sources:

  • American Psychological Association (2001). Publication manual of the American Psychological Association (5th ed.). Washington, D.C.: Author
  • American Psychological Association (2007), APA style guide to electronic resources. Washington, D.C.: Author
  • APA website: http://apastyle.apa.org/
